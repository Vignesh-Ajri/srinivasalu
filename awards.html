Recognition

Honours &
Awards

A distinguished career recognized by national and international bodies — from Guinness World Records to state honours.

2016
State Honour

State Environment Award

Conferred by the Honorable Chief Minister of Karnataka on June 5th, World Environment Day, in recognition of outstanding contributions to environmental protection and forest conservation.

2016
Tourism

Best Tourism Award by FKCCI

Conferred for outstanding activities promoting tourism in Dandeli. Presented by the Honorable Tourism Minister for transforming Dandeli into a premier eco-tourism destination.

2016
Wildlife Tourism

Holiday IQ Award & Dr. Chuck McDougal Memorial Award

Runner-up for the Dr. Chuck McDougal Memorial Award (TOFT Wildlife Tourism Awards) for creating a visitor-friendly wildlife destination. Holiday IQ Award for best tourism and hospitality at Kali Tiger Reserve.

2016
International

Shanthi Samsara — PM Modi, COP21 Paris

Documentary footage on climate change and clean energy featured by Hon'ble Prime Minister Narendra Modi at the COP 21 Summit in Paris — bringing global attention to India's forest conservation efforts.

2016
Film Production

Short Movie Production — ADCL

Produced a short movie documenting the inspiring success stories of the Dr. B.R. Ambedkar Development Corporation, showcasing the impact of welfare programmes on Dalit communities across Karnataka.

2017
Technology

Mobile App "Kalyana" Launch

Developed the "Kalyana" mobile application for the Social Welfare Department, Government of Karnataka. Launched by Shri Rahul Gandhi Ji on 21st July 2017 at the Dr. B.R. Ambedkar International Conference, Bangalore.

2021
Record

Asia Book of Records

Asia Book of Records recognition for the largest number of eco-friendly Turmeric Ganesh idols — a landmark KSPCB initiative for an eco-friendly Ganesh festival in 2021, promoting environmental consciousness.

2022
Environment

H. V. MuniswamyGowda Environment Award

Awarded for efficient performance and significant contributions to environmental protection in Karnataka.

2022
Guinness World Record

Guinness World Record: Largest Online Photo Album of Handmade Sculpture

Guinness World Record for the largest online photo album featuring handmade sculptures, achieved through a mass participation initiative under KSPCB.

2022
Guinness World Record

Guinness World Record: Most People Sculpting with Modelling Clay Simultaneously

Guinness World Record for the most people sculpting with modelling clay simultaneously — a landmark community engagement achievement under KSPCB's eco-awareness programme.
